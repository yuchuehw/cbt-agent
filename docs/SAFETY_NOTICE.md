# Safety Notice

This project is an experimental CBT-style conversational system for educational and research purposes.

## Important limitations

- This software is not a licensed mental health professional.
- This software is not a medical device.
- This software does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.
- This software is not a substitute for emergency or clinical care.

## Crisis and emergency situations

Do not rely on this software in an emergency.

If you may harm yourself or others, contact local emergency services immediately.
If you are in the US or Canada, call or text `988` (Suicide and Crisis Lifeline).
If you are elsewhere, contact your local crisis hotline or emergency number.

## Human oversight requirement

Any use in support settings should include qualified human oversight.
You are responsible for reviewing outputs before acting on them.

## High-risk use warning

Outputs can be incorrect, incomplete, biased, or inappropriate.
Do not use this software as the sole basis for health, legal, safety, or other high-impact decisions.

## Age and vulnerable users

This project is not designed or approved for unsupervised use by minors.
Use with vulnerable populations requires appropriate professional supervision.

## No guarantee of safety outcomes

Guardrails reduce risk but do not eliminate risk.
No representation is made that all harmful outputs are prevented.
